## üí° **CAMPAIGN STRATEGY**

### **Core Principles:**

1. **Tiered Approach**: Different messages and offers based on:
   - Customer tier (Super VIP > VIP > Regular)
   - Days inactive (30-60d ‚Üí 120-180d)
   
2. **Escalating Incentives**: The longer they've been gone, the bigger the offer

3. **Personalization**: Use their name, reference past purchases, create urgency

4. **Test & Learn**: A/B test messages, track response rates, iterate

5. **Multi-Touch**: SMS ‚Üí Email ‚Üí Phone call (for Super VIPs)

## üéØ **POST-CAMPAIGN STRATEGY**

### **If Customer Returns:**
1. **Welcome them back warmly** - make them feel missed
2. **Ask for feedback**: "What brought you back?"
3. **Capture email/phone** if missing
4. **Offer loyalty reward**: "Visit 3 more times this month, get X"
5. **Set follow-up reminder**: Text them in 2 weeks

### **If Customer Doesn't Respond:**
- **Wait 90 days**, then try one final "We're removing you" message
- **Remove from active marketing** after 180 days total
- **Archive customer** but keep in database for future win-backs

### **Ongoing Retention Program:**
- **30-day check-in**: If customer hasn't visited in 30 days, send friendly reminder
- **Birthday/Anniversary**: Special offers on customer milestones
- **Loyalty tiers**: Make it clear what perks each tier gets
- **VIP exclusives**: Early access to new products, private events

---

## üí° **OPTIMIZATION TIPS**

### **A/B Testing Ideas:**
1. **Emoji vs. No Emoji** in messages
2. **Discount % vs. Free Product** offers
3. **Urgency** ("48 hours" vs. "This weekend")
4. **Personal** ("We miss you") vs. **Transactional** ("Here's an offer")
5. **Send time**: Tuesday 11am vs. Friday 2pm vs. Saturday 12pm

### **Personalization Enhancements:**
- Reference their **favorite strain** (from purchase history)
- Mention **last product purchased**
- Show **lifetime value** (for VIPs: "You've spent $X with us!")
- **Days since last visit** (create urgency)
- **Budtender name** if they always saw the same person

### **Offer Optimization:**
- **Test discount levels**: 10% vs. 15% vs. 20%
- **Test free products**: Pre-roll vs. 1/8th vs. Vape
- **Test exclusivity**: "VIP-only offer" vs. generic
- **Test scarcity**: "Limited to first 50" vs. open-ended
